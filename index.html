Our Story

Minute Motion Creations, headquartered in Thane, Mumbai, is a premier production house dedicated to creating films and web series that resonate globally. We blend passion, innovation, and cinematic excellence to tell stories that inspire and provoke thought.

Vision: To redefine global cinema with universal narratives that transcend borders.

Mission: To deliver bold, high-quality content that pushes creative boundaries and sets new industry standards.

Values: Artistry, Authenticity, Collaboration, Excellence.

Founded by a dynamic team of filmmakers, writers, and creatives, our studio leverages collective expertise to shape the future of cinematic storytelling. Our team includes award-winning directors, skilled cinematographers, and passionate storytellers committed to excellence.

From concept to screen, we collaborate with global talent to bring diverse stories to life, ensuring every project reflects our commitment to quality and innovation.
